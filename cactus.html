The May-Treanor Room - A Unique Olympic Experience


Step into the extraordinary with our specially designed room,
"The May-Treanor Room," a testament to our commitment to creating
unique and memorable experiences. Named after the legendary athlete,
Misty May-Treanor, this room is a celebration of her achievements in beach volleyball.

From beach-inspired aesthetics every element is selected to transport you into
the world of this Olympic icon.
By choosing "The May-Treanor Room," you not only enjoy a one-of-a-kind stay but also become an integral part of a distinctive Olympic narrative.
